Welcome to Quran o Tarbiyah in an Interactive Way

Quranotarbiyah understands that raising children with a strong foundation in divine knowledge is a significant responsibility for every Muslim parent. While this responsibility primarily lies with the parents, we at Quranotarbiyah are dedicated to sharing this important duty with the Muslim community. Our commitment is not just a claim; we genuinely understand its significance. We take full responsibility for ensuring excellent Quranic education outcomes.

Our approach to achieving these exceptional results involves slow but carefully planned steps that ensure a deep understanding of Quranic verses. By guiding your child step by step, we help them reach the highest level where they can read the Quran online with Tajweed.

We not only guarantee high-quality learning but also prioritize your comfort. Learning the Quran becomes a lot easier with us, thanks to the convenience of home-based learning, flexible timings, parental supervision, and the guidance of expert Quran teachers. Quranotarbiyah is committed to providing an exceptional learning experience.

A few reasons why we might be the right fit

Round-the-Clock Learning Support

We offer 24/7 service for Quran learning, providing continuous support to help students learn quickly and smoothly.

Personalized Online Quran Sessions

Experience one-on-one online Quran learning sessions tailored to you and your family's needs, ensuring a comfortable and effective learning journey.

Expert Female Tutors

Benefit from our highly qualified and dedicated female tutors, committed to transmitting the essence of the Holy Quran with care and expertise.

We've tailored our courses to accommodate the diverse needs of our esteemed students. Whether they're children taking their first steps in Quranic education or adults seeking to deepen their understanding, we've crafted specialized courses with their age and abilities in mind.

For children and beginners, we offer specialized courses that commence with Noorani Qaida, a renowned guide for Quranic reading fundamentals. Adults, on the other hand, require a distinct approach, which we meticulously provide. Our emphasis on Tajweed is unwavering, integrated seamlessly from the outset. Our teachers diligently assist students in mastering proper pronunciation from day one, fostering an intuitive grasp of Tajweed as they progress. To make our students comprehend and implement the teachings of the Quran we also have an additional course in Tafseer to understand the meaning of the verses from the Holy Quran.

Our Quran Courses

We've introduced a Quran reading course tailored specifically for adults, which has garnered acclaim among our older learners.

Our experienced online Quran tutors meticulously guide students through the Quran, focusing on word-by-word comprehension to ensure effective learning.

Ample time is allocated for students to absorb new Quranic vocabulary, ensuring a thorough understanding.

We also offer an online course in learning the Arabic language to help in understanding the Quran in a better way and to improve one’s linguistic and writing skills in Arabic.

Our Approach and Additional Courses

We provide Tarbiyah with Sunnah lessons based on Islamic teachings from the ‘Seerah’ of our beloved Prophet (SAW) and from the Quranic scriptures.

Understanding the unique dynamics of online teaching, we've structured our courses to foster an optimal virtual learning environment.

All our Quranic courses are conducted on a one-to-one basis, allowing for personalized attention and ensuring students remain fully engaged throughout their lessons.

We cater to various age groups, learning methodologies, and preferences, offering a comprehensive range of courses including Noorani Qaida, Quran Reading, Advanced Tajweed, Learning Arabic Language, Quranic Translation (Tafseer) and Lessons on Tarbiyah.
